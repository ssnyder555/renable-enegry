Investing in Micro-Infrastructure

Most of the infrastructure that supports some of the largest cities in the country is outdated and susceptible to damage and in most cases continue to increase energy charges despite the lack of upgrades needed to handle a cleaner future. We have a government failing to act on urgency. Austin Renewable is a chance to help individuals shrink their carbon footprint, but also to help communities come together and take ownership of the utilities and energy production, strengthen neighborhoods during times of need and help put money into your pockets as an investor. Joining this cooperative will allow Austin to accelerate it’s transition to renewable energy and helping change the world for the better.

Community Sun Gardens 4

Go solar without needing to install solar panels on your home. Sun gardens do not have to be connected to your home in order to benefit. You do not even have to own your home. This will be done with zero real estate required. Change your address and take your share in solar energy program with you! As a subscriber, Austin Renewable will produce energy and feed it to the grid on your behalf through Power Purchase Agreements, which will result in credits on your electric bill. Or create a portfolio and monitor and earn residuals for your energy. Because the solar array is on the ground and at one location instead of the roofs of each individual home across the city, we can reduce our initial expenses and increase our output longterm.

Hydrogen Production

Hydrogen gas production using state of the art technology to produce clean and safe energy to supplement our solar array. This process allows us to increase clean sustainable output and generate passive income for members.!
